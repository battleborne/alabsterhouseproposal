01 · Situation Analysis

Where the Current Setup Falls Short

The Leadership Summit has exceptional programming, credentialed speakers, and a mission-aligned audience. The operational infrastructure hasn't kept pace — and with the May 19 date approaching, that gap is a real risk.

```
Risk

No Automated Follow-Up

Registrations flow into Google Sheets with no triggered confirmations, reminders, or logistics emails. Every message is sent manually or not at all.

Risk

No SMS Infrastructure

With a high-capacity audience including executives and legislators, text is the highest-open channel. There is currently no opt-in or messaging system in place.

Risk

No Attendee Segmentation

Individual ticket holders, Table Hosts, and $40K Premier Sponsors all receive the same communication — or none. Tier-specific messaging doesn't exist.

Opportunity

Existing List Is an Asset

The Google Sheets attendee list is a clean foundation. With the right connectors it becomes a fully automated, segmented communications engine in under 48 hours.

Opportunity

High-Value Audience, Low Retention Tools

SEC Commissioners, Members of Congress, and C-suite executives attend — but there is no structured way to retain them, deepen their relationship, or market next year's event.

Opportunity

Natural Membership Framework

Roundtable topics, Capitol access, and exclusive programming are naturally tiered. A named membership program formalizes the value that already exists.

```

04 · Audience Growth

Four Tactics That Compound Over Time

These are low-cost, high-leverage moves that build attendance year-over-year without paid advertising.

```
01

Alumni Referral Program

Every post-event email includes a unique referral link. Attendees who bring a new registrant receive $50 toward next year. Tracked automatically in Mailchimp via tagging — no manual tracking needed.

02

Roundtable Topic Communities

13 roundtable topics = 13 micro-audience segments. Tag attendees by topic interest. Send one relevant, substantive email per quarter. Keeps relationships warm year-round without a major content lift.

03

Next Generation Pricing Track

Formalize the "bring a future leader" message already on the site: any paid attendee may bring one person under 35 at 50% off. Widens your audience age profile and builds a decade-long attendee pipeline.

04

LinkedIn Amplification

Post-summit, send all attendees a pre-written, 1-click copyable LinkedIn post about their DC experience. High-capacity executives sharing authentic Capitol Hill content is free, organic reach to exactly the right audience.

```
